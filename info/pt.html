Public Transport

Ceremony

If you're planning on PT'ing it to the ceremony, the 75 tram goes past Charles street and Burwood station (on the Alamein line) is a moderate walk away.

Reception

From the church, take the 75 tram back towards the city and change to a 78 tram at Church street. Alight at stop 55 (Gibbons street) and stroll to Church Street Enoteca.

Of course, there are always taxis...
